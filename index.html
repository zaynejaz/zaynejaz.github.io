Production Process
Scope & Strategy Definition
Define the email marketing campaign strategy and its goals in order to align the campaigns direction and accurately measure successes or failures, and learn from them.
Content Creation
Having a message tailored to the target audience coupled with relatable, positive language will ensure a clear, understandable call-to-action be conveyed.
Email Design
Along with Content, an Email's design is what will capture a reader's eye, making this is one of, if not the most important part of the email production process.
Email Development
Developing a memorable and unique user experience is vital. This is achieved with accurate, clean, creative code used to translate designs into a user interface.
Quality Assurance Testing
Testing on a variety of elements is done to ensure the email works as intended over a range of devices, systems and email clients with the use of Litmus or Emails on Acid.
Approvals
Once all checks have been carried out and errors corrected, the email is approved and all relative information/lessons learned is/are recorded and filed accordingly.
Launch
With all the proper quality assurances & approvals in place and carried out correctly, sending the email can be done with full confidence that the campaign will meet the project's strategy.
Post-Send Analysis
Here we will see if and how our goals were met with regard to the email's scope and strategy as per the project's definition to it's metrics (eg. Bounce, Click, Open or Unsubscribe rates, complaints, etc)
